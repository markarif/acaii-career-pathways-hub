Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer? Submit a question below and get a reply within 24 hours.

Do I need a degree to access these opportunities? +
Not necessarily. Some programmes require a degree or specific academic background, but many — including most mentorship and training opportunities — are open to anyone with a genuine interest in AI and a problem they want to solve. We always list the requirements clearly on each opportunity page.
I am not a technical person. Can I still benefit from AI opportunities? +
Absolutely. AI innovation needs more than coders — it needs problem-solvers, community connectors, health workers, farmers, and educators who understand the real challenges. Many of our most successful innovators came from non-technical backgrounds. If you have a problem and a community, we can help you find technical support.
How quickly will I hear back after submitting a question or application? +
Questions submitted through this hub are answered within 24 hours on weekdays. For formal programme applications, timelines vary by programme — all deadlines and response windows are listed on each opportunity card. Our AI assistant handles common questions instantly, day or night.
Can I apply if I am outside Kenya? +
The Hub is currently focused on Kenya, but we are expanding. Several of our opportunities — including the AI4D Innovation Scaling Challenge and AI4D Scholarships — are open to applicants across sub-Saharan Africa. Each opportunity clearly states the eligible countries. If you are in East Africa and cannot find a relevant opportunity, contact us anyway — we may be able to make a referral to a partner organisation.
Is there a cost to use this Hub? +
No. The Africa AI Career Pathways Hub is completely free to use. There are no application fees, registration fees, or subscription costs. ACAII-ACTS AI Institute is funded by development partners specifically to make these opportunities accessible to people who would otherwise not know they exist.
What is ACAII-ACTS AI Institute and who runs this Hub? +
ACAII-ACTS AI Institute (Africa Centre for Advanced Innovation Initiatives — AI and Technology for Society) is a nonprofit organisation supporting AI innovators across Africa. We work with funders including FCDO, AI4D Africa, and GIZ to connect talented individuals and early-stage innovators with the resources they need to build and scale solutions to African challenges.
